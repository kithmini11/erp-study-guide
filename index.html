Purpose: Making stuff

Purpose: Perfect stuff

💾 Three Types of Data in ERP

🗃️ Master Data

Nature: Static & Shared

Examples:

  • Customer Master
  • Vendor Master
  • Material Master
  • Employee Master
  • Chart of Accounts

Usage: Reference across processes

📋 Transactional Data

Nature: Dynamic & Time-sensitive

Examples:

  • Sales Orders
  • Purchase Orders
  • Invoices
  • Payments
  • Goods Receipts

Usage: Daily operations

📈 Analytical Data

Nature: Derived & Aggregated

Examples:

  • Sales Trends
  • Customer Insights
  • KPIs
  • Profitability Reports
  • Forecasting Data

Usage: Strategic decisions

Purchase Requisition (PR)

Internal request for materials/services

Methods: Manual or Automatic (MRP, Maintenance, Production)

Request for Quotation (RFQ)

Get quotes from multiple vendors

Process: Create RFQ → Enter quotes → Compare → Select vendor

Purchase Order (PO)

Formal request to vendor

Types: Local, Import, Subcontracting, Service, CAPEX, STO

Goods Receipt

Record received materials

Impact: Material document (qty) + Accounting document (value)

Movement Type: 101

🤝 Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Pipeline

Memory Aid: "Leads Love Opportunities, Quotes Create Customers"

🎯 Leads

Potential customers showing interest

Website forms, inquiries, social media

💡 Opportunities

Qualified leads with buying intent

BANT qualified (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)

📄 Quotations

Formal pricing documents

Products, prices, terms, validity

👥 Customers

Established business relationships

Purchased or contracted

Lead Management

Source: Website, stores, social media, email

Status: Not yet qualified

Opportunity Management

Criteria: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline

Activities: Demos, meetings, proposals

Quote Management

Includes: Products, quantities, prices, terms

Features: Validity period, payment terms

Customer Management

Data: Purchase history, preferences, interactions

Integration: Links to sales and support

🔄 Key Integration Points

MM ↔ FI Integration

Connection: Invoice Verification

Purchase orders create financial commitments, goods receipts update material values

MM ↔ PP Integration

Connection: Material Requirements

Production orders trigger material procurement, BOM determines requirements

SD ↔ MM Integration

Connection: Availability Check

Sales orders check inventory, customer orders may trigger procurement

HR ↔ All Modules

Connection: Employee Master

Authorization workflows, cost center assignments, organizational structure
